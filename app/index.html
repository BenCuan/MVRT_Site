We are MVRT

Our Team

MVRT is a student run high school robotics team which builds a new robot every year for the FIRST Robotics Competition. The robot must be built in 6 weeks and must adhere to the rules of competiton of the given year .

  • About Us

The Competition

Every year the FIRST organizaiton holds a high school robotics competiton in which all teams must build a robot to compete in the competiton. The competiton is a a score based game which has different rules every year. Vist the games page for more details.
