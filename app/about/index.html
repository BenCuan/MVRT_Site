Since 1997, MVRT has been the premier Engineering organization on campus. We work to promote STEM at school, in the community, and abroad. MVRT educates our students in Engineering, but also how to run an organization, including outreach and finance. MVRT teaches our members the skills necessary to succeed in STEM and other fields
